Travel

Chancellors Hotel

The hotel is roughly 3 miles south of the city centre.

Train

If you are travelling by train, the hotel is only 15 minutes taxi ride for Manchester Picadilly station. You could also change at Picadilly station and get a train to Mauldeth Road which is 5 minutes walk from the venue.

There are roughly three trains an hour from London Euston. To find detailed information click here.

Air Travel

If you are coming from overseas, Manchester Airport is a 15 minutes taxi ride (8 miles) from the hotel. There are also regular trains to Manchester Picadilly from the airport.
